**Reliable platforms where you can give free mock interviews** — including both peer-to-peer and expert-led options — for **software engineering roles**, especially backend/system design/LLD/DSA:

---

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **1. Pramp**

**Website:** [https://www.pramp.com](https://www.pramp.com)

* **Type:** Peer-to-peer mock interviews
* **Topics:** DSA, System Design, Behavioral
* **Features:** Real-time coding + video call + structured feedback
* **Free:** ✅ Completely free

---

### 👨‍🏫 **2. Interviewing.io**

**Website:** [https://www.interviewing.io](https://www.interviewing.io)

* **Type:** Anonymous mock interviews with engineers from top companies (Google, Meta, etc.)
* **Topics:** DSA, System Design, Behavioral
* **Free:** ✅ Yes (after eligibility check or sign-up wait)

---

### 🤖 **3. Exponent (Free Tier)**

**Website:** [https://www.tryexponent.com/interview-prep/mock-interview](https://www.tryexponent.com/interview-prep/mock-interview)

* **Type:** Peer and AI-based mock interviews
* **Topics:** System Design, Behavioral, PM, Coding
* **Free:** ⚠️ Limited sessions for free users

---

### 🌐 **4. LeetCode Mock Interview (Lite)**

**Website:** [https://leetcode.com/mock-interview/](https://leetcode.com/mock-interview/)

* **Type:** Simulated real interview (no video)
* **Topics:** DSA (Timed)
* **Free:** ✅ Basic version is free; Premium gives more

---

### 🧠 **5. Tech Mock Interview Discords / Reddit**

**Communities:**

* [r/cscareerquestions](https://www.reddit.com/r/cscareerquestions)

* [r/mockinterview](https://www.reddit.com/r/mockinterview/)

* Discord servers like:

  * Blind 75 Discord
  * LeetCode Grinding Group

* **Type:** Schedule peer mock interviews

* **Free:** ✅ Yes

---

### 💬 **6. Meet a Mentor (Coding Ninjas / LinkedIn)**

* Many mentors offer **free mock interviews via LinkedIn** or communities like [https://www.codementor.io](https://www.codementor.io)
* Join **Coding Ninjas Studio** or **Scaler Discords** to find volunteers
